## Học thuật


### **Slide 1: The Glance (Landscape)**
- **The Goal:** A high-level market snapshot.
- **Content:** A visual overview of the industry, market size, and key players. Avoid dry statistics; use evocative keywords and imagery to set the scene.  

### **Slide 2: The Glimmering (Visionaries)**
- **The Goal:** Inspiration.
- **Content:** Emerging trends, "signals" of the future, or disruptive case studies that are currently breaking the mold within the industry.

### **Slide 3: The Gaps (The Void)**
- **The Goal:** Identifying the "missing pieces."
- **Content:** Unmet consumer needs, market vacuums, or systemic bottlenecks that current technology or processes have failed to resolve.  

### **Slide 4: The Grit (The Hard Truth)**
- **The Goal:** Building Empathy.
- **Content:** The "unfiltered" reality. Cultural barriers, cost constraints, or the "behind-the-scenes" struggles that only those "in the game" truly understand.

### **Slide 5: The Guts (The Brave Challenge)**
- **The Goal:** Call to Action (The Pitch).
- **Content:**
    - A specific, localized problem or a daring hypothetical challenge.
    - What's is first thought on how solution should looks like?
